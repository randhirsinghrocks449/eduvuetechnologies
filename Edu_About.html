About Us

eduVue is an Education Institute Management System(EIMS) which is designed on a modern, cloud based, multi-tenant architecture. We are a CMM Level 5 certified technology company.We are engaged in providing Technology to Academic Instituions to convert their offline business to online.

Students, faculty and staff members of the institute now can perform all their duties and many more activities through a single window which includes web, mobile and other devices. It can be anywhere as you like, such as any geography or any devices of your choice.

Our Mission

To Build Foolproof solutions for Academic Instituions by leveraging best technology and increase its adoption among stakeholder.
